About GlobeTrek Adventures

Your trusted partner in exploring the world since 2012

Our Story

Founded in 2012 in the beautiful coastal city of Negombo, Sri Lanka, GlobeTrek Adventures began with a simple mission: to make extraordinary travel experiences accessible to everyone.

What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Sri Lanka's most trusted travel agencies, serving over 50,000 happy travelers from around the world.

Our deep local knowledge combined with global partnerships allows us to craft unique journeys that go beyond typical tourist trails.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To create transformative travel experiences that connect people with cultures, nature, and themselves while promoting sustainable and responsible tourism.

Vision: To become the leading travel partner in South Asia, recognized for exceptional service, authentic experiences, and positive community impact.
